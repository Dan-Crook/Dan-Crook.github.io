Dice Game

win or lose

Rules

You must select cards that when added together equal the total of both dice added together.

For Example: If you rolled a 2 and a 4 The total rolled would be 6
Therefore possible options could be:
card 6
cards 5 and 1
cards 3, 2 and 1 etc..

Once selected, Submit your answer.

To Win

To win the Game, you must use all cards.
If the total of the dice cannot be made by the cards you have left then you Lose!
